ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Jessica Johnson and I'm a Graphic Designer born and bred in Sydney.

I'm passionate about simple and clean designs that push ideas beyond the norm and explore new ways of thinking and doing.

I believe that the integrity and success of design depends heavily on the attention given to the details therein, and this is what I try to achieve in every design.
