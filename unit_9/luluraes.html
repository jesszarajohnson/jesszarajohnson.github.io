The Lu Lu Raes Single Cover


The Sydney band The LuLu Raes have just realised their new single - Infinite Paradise. The single art cover reflects the sun-soaked, psychedelic, pop song through the illustration of the fluid lines and vibrant array of colours. I tried to demonstrate the title -Infinite Paradise by using a black background combined with bright colours representing the cosmic universe. Together with the linking and looping typography which showcases the never-ending theme.
