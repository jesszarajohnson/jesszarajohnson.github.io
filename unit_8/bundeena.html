Bundeena Coast Eco Lodge


Bundeena Coast is a recreation camp for eco-tourism, situated next to the Royal National Park. The branding reflects its environmental awareness through vibrant earthy tones and rounded shapes matching the nature in which the camp is situated. As the client requested to highlight the Agophora tree, it has become the motif of the brand though an abstract icon as well as dynamic images of the tree.
