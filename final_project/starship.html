Sydney Starship and The Pontoon Wedding brochures


The Starship and The Pontoon are luxury cruising vessels designed for all different entertaining purposes, navigating around the stunning Sydney Harbour. I was tasked with updating and modernising their wedding packages to show off both the vessels features. Sophistication and elegance was the focus of the design with plenty of images to allow the guests to easily imagine what it would be like for them on their wedding day.
